Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page describes how the official Gates of Chaos website handles visitors' personal data and which external tools are used. We manage the site ourselves, without using analytics or advertising tracking systems.

Data controller

Gates of Chaos, Rome (Italy). For any request regarding your data, you can write to gatesofchaosband@gmail.com.

Data collected

The site does not use profiling cookies or analytics/tracking tools. We do not automatically collect visitors' personal data.

Newsletter

The newsletter signup form opens your email client with a pre-filled message addressed to us: the address you send is manually added to our contact list. You can request removal at any time by writing to the address above.

Third-party services

The site integrates a few external services, each subject to its own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts — the site's typefaces are loaded from Google's servers. This means your IP address is sent to Google when the page loads.
  • Spotify — the players embedded in the Music section are loaded only if you click the play button, not when the page loads. Once activated, Spotify may set its own cookies according to its policy.
  • Bandcamp, Facebook, Instagram — these links lead to independent external platforms, each with its own privacy policy.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your data (for example your email in our contact list) at any time, by writing to gatesofchaosband@gmail.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated in the future, for example if an online store or traffic analysis tools are added. In that case the update date at the top of the page will be changed accordingly.
